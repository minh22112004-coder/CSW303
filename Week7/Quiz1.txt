1) The primary purpose of a software process model is to:
A. Force all developers to work the same way.
B. Provide structure and guidance to the software development lifecycle.
C. Remove the need for customer involvement.
D. Guarantee zero defects.
Answer: B

2) Which analogy BEST represents the importance of having a software process:
A. Taking an exam without studying.
B. Cooking a meal by following a recipe.
C. Listening to music while working.
D. Shopping without a budget.
Answer: B

3) Which of the following is the most important reason why software process models differ across projects:
A. Developer personal preferences.
B. Differences in customer hobbies.
C. Variation in project uncertainty and requirements stability.
D. Availability of office equipment.
Answer: C

4) Early software processes were heavily influenced by:
A. Social media platforms.
B. Manufacturing and engineering disciplines.
C. Modern agile startups.
D. Open-source community collaboration.
Answer: B

5) A software process helps improve product quality primarily by:
A. Standardizing development activities.
B. Reducing team communication.
C. Preventing all requirement changes.
D. Eliminating the need for testing.
Answer: A

6) A process without defined stages or structure usually leads to:
A. High predictability.
B. Consistent product quality.
C. Chaos and miscommunication.
D. Reduced development time.
Answer: C

7) Which of the following is considered a major category of software process models:
A. Sequential vs Functional.
B. Predictive vs Adaptive.
C. Horizontal vs Vertical.
D. Internal vs External.
Answer: B

8) Which situation most strongly demands a structured software process:
A. A small assignment done by one student.
B. A large project with multiple teams and responsibilities.
C. A fun side project made for entertainment.
D. A quick prototype for personal learning.
Answer: B

9) One of the central challenges that drove the evolution of software processes was:
A. Hardware prices decreasing.
B. Increasing complexity and changing requirements.
C. Fewer people wanting to be developers.
D. Overabundance of technical documentation.
Answer: B

10) Which statement best describes the role of a process model:
A. It guarantees the fastest possible development.
B. It ensures that all teams worldwide use identical workflows.
C. It provides a structured approach for producing software.
D. It eliminates the need for planning.
Answer: C
